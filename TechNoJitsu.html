Welcome to TechnoJitsu!





Technojitsu is an open source code project for beginners as well as experienced programmers meant to pick up as well as cultivate interest in computers and its many major roles played in present times.


This platform provides a variety of features for related parties which facilitates interaction between contemporary thinkers with contemporary times so as to be kept well informed in a fast paced world.

Projects

A platform to upload projects with simple and precise animations on real time electronic applications for a better learning and understanding experience of the same and to be an inspiration for better and more practical projects which can be used to ease our everyday lives.

Articles

Articles of the contemporary discoveries and thoughts which decides the path taken in future or the updates on tech or gizmos which could make one have a head start in their professional lives or have secure knowledge of one’s personal life’s privacy and security.

Programming-Lang-Guide

A look into the workings of software/program languages, on the basic but essential knowledge which one should know so as to enter the world of data and applications on which we are deliberately dependent upon in our day to day lives as ones sees through software like google maps, notes, calendar etc.
